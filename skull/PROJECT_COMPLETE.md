## üåü PROJECT HIGHLIGHTS

### The Story
**William the Wildheart**, a smuggler from 18th century England, was betrayed by his family and executed. His ghost now haunts his estate, protecting a hidden treasure. You must navigate through underground tunnels, dark forests, and the Gothic mansion to claim the treasure - but beware, succeeding means inheriting William's curse!

### Game Features
- 10 playable locations
- 4 collectible items (lamp, shovel, potion, treasure)
- Multiple room descriptions
- Inventory management
- Winning ending with story twist
- Interactive and scripted play modes

### Original Game Scope
- 100+ locations (from extracted data)
- Ship areas (cargo hold)
- Full mansion (multiple floors and rooms)
- Beach/coastal areas
- Medieval sections
- Current playable version covers ~10% of original content
